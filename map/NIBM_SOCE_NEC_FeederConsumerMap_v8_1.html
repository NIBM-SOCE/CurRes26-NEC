Feeder and Consumer Map - Applied Cybersecurity (Network Engineering and Cybersecurity)

Vertical mapping of the SLQF Level 3 (Diploma) and Level 4 (HND) stages onto the validated Coventry University FHEQ Levels 5 and 6. Year 3 (Level 5) is shared in full; Year 4 (Level 6) branches into the Ethical Hacking and Network Engineering tracks. Every module is placed by what prepares it and what it prepares.

Award: BSc (Hons) Applied Cybersecurity (Ethical Hacking and Network Engineering tracks) HND exit: Higher National Diploma in Network Engineering and Cybersecurity Validation partner: Coventry University Architecture: v8.1 (module records v1.0) Credit basis: SLQF - 1 credit = 50 notional hours (Industrial Training = 100) Working language: Python-first with Bash, SQL support Diploma exit: 41 credits / 13 modules  ·  HND exit: 43 credits / 15 modules Status: Governance submission
