## 3. Module Purpose and Aims

Industrial Training is where the programme's competencies meet a real workplace. Over an approximately twelve-week full-time placement, scheduled outside teaching weeks, the student applies the two years of networking, security and systems competency in a professional setting, evidences professional practice through a maintained log and an employer-facing engagement, and reflects on the placement learning in a report defended at viva. The module teaches no subject content: its substance is the placement itself and the auditable evidence it produces.

Any placement involving security-sensitive work inherits the pathway's ethical and legal expectations, and placement scope is confirmed with the host before commencement.

## 6. Assessment

Components per the common frame and the Assessment Component Taxonomy (04_Conventions.md v1.2). Grading mode and weightings are thread-proposed here and confirmed at sign-off; any pass-fail component is justified against the TVEC competency-evidence orientation.

| Component code | Task | Attributes | ILOs | Weight (proposed) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| CW-I-Home | Placement completion and individually maintained placement log: the completion record with the maintained log evidencing professional practice across the placement; oral vehicle: None | Individual; take-home (placement-based); graded; oral vehicle: None | LO1, LO2 | 40 |
| CW-I-Home | Placement report: the reflective placement report evidencing and reflecting on the placement learning; oral vehicle: None | Individual; take-home; graded; oral vehicle: None | LO2, LO3 | 35 |
| CW-I-Class | Closing Viva-defence: the placement defended at viva against the log and report; oral vehicle: Viva-defence (mandatory) | Individual; in-class (viva); graded; oral vehicle: Viva-defence (mandatory) | LO1, LO3 | 25 |

Weights sum to 100 (proposed). Placement completion is a precondition of the award of the module (frame): the completion requirement is proposed as a pass-fail gate on LO1, justified against the TVEC competency-evidence orientation, with the log, report and viva graded as above. Reassessment of a failed graded component is by an equivalent task per School regulations; a failed placement is repeated per placement regulations. All assessment under the academic integrity policy including its generative-AI provisions; module-level AI stance at teaching-plan stage. The log, report and viva record are auditable competency evidence oriented to TVEC; TVEC requirements are not yet final and additional requirements may be introduced.
